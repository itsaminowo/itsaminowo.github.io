Welcome to FindOut Search

• On FindOut Search, you can find somewhat useful information on a variety of topics and people.

How do I use FindOut Search?

• You may be thinking; how do I use this? Well all you have to do is is click or search for something that may interest you on the navigation bar above and get started reading!

Tooltips: Knowing what's what

• When you hover over an item (eg. a header), you will be able to see what that item is, along with it's directory.

What are locked pages?

• A locked page is a page that is either essential (Admin Page) to FindOut Search or is a legacy page.

• A legacy page can be about or designated to a topic or person that is general and seems to go into public interest very often.

• The opposite of a legacy page is a neutral page. Neutral pages can be about or designated to a topic or person that is not of the current public interest. Neutral pages are unlocked.
